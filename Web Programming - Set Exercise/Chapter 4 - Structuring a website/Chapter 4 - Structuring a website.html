SAVANNA

Savannas are often described as an area of grasslands characterized by a mixture of grasses, trees, and shrubs. The typical experiences of climate are warm temperature with wet and dry seasons. There are four Savannas found in various regions around the world, including Africa, South America, Central America and, Southern Asia. Savanna is home to many different wildlife, including large herbivores like elephants, zebras, and giraffes, as well as predators like lions, leopards, and cheetahs.
