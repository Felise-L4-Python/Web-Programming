MANGO GRAHAM

Prep time: 30 mins | Refrigerate overnight: 12 hours

Mango graham is a popular dessert in the Philippines and one of the Filipino's favorite desserts to eat. It is made with layers of graham crackers, fresh mango slices, and sweetened cream. It is a refreshing and satisfying treat that is perfect for any occasion, from casual gatherings to special celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (370g) Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Fresh Milk
  • 2 packs (400g) Graham Crackers
  • 2 packs (250ml) All-Purpose Cream
  • 2 Mangoes (thinly sliced, save the excess Mango flesh)

Procedure

  1. Put 2 packs of all-purpose cream and 1 sweetened condensed milk in the bowl and whisk or use hand mixer to beat.
  2. Then add the excess Mango flesh in the bowl and mix it well, make sure to crushed the Mangoes to have smooth consistency.
  3. Spread some of the cream mixture in your container and make it evenly spread.
  4. Grab another bowl and pour 1 cup of fresh milk in the bowl and dip the graham crackers in.
  5. Once the graham crackers were dipped in fresh milk, layer the graham crackers into the containter. Then pour some cream mixture to your graham crackers and make sure all crackers are covered.
  6. Then layer it down with the thinly sliced mangoes.
  7. Repeat until you reach your desired Mango Graham height and crushed at least 3 graham crackers and sprinkle it on top.
  8. Chill for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.
